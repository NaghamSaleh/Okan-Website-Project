About Us

University of Distinction Fostering Global Leaders.

Okan University is one of the leading Universities in Istanbul; it was established in 1999 by Okan Culture, Education, and Sports Foundation. Okan University focuses on practical learning besides academic education, so it has many training centers like the Learning Application and Research Center, Culinary Arts Center, and Continuing Education Center. The university aims to contribute to the development and improvement of society by providing education of the highest standards alongside training students to be fully prepared to enter the modern business world. Okan University includes diverse programs (Medical, Engineering, Administrative, Social Sciences, and Arts) 63 undergraduate, 41 master, and 13 Ph. D. programs under ten faculties, two vocational schools, and three graduate schools.
